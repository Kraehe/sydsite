Sydney Millevoi is a Pop and R&B singer- songwriter based in New York / New Jersey.

Sydney Millevoi has sung all her life but only started writing at 18. She strengthens her pen by day and exercises her voice at night as a wedding singer for Silver Pro Entertainment.

As a Season 22 contestant on American Idol, Sydney has had the opportunity to grow her artistry in front of global music sensations: Katy Perry, Lionel Richie, and Luke Bryan.
